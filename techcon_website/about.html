Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for software engineers and has grown into a global conference attracting thousands of attendees from around the world. Over the years, it has become a platform for launching startups, showcasing innovations, and shaping the future of technology.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to inspire innovation, build community, and drive progress in the tech industry. We bring together brilliant minds to share ideas, solve real-world problems, and empower the next generation of technologists.

Notable Past Speakers

  • Dr. Aisha Mwangeka – AI researcher and 2022 keynote speaker. Known for her work on ethical AI and machine learning for healthcare.

  • Carlos Rivera – CTO of CodeGlobal. Shared insights in 2021 on scaling cloud infrastructure for startups.

  • Lin Zhang – Cybersecurity expert and 2020 panelist. Advocated for privacy-first technology design in global systems.
